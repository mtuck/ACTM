Divisions

*Division 1:

The Division One competition is for public schools which are large enough to be classified as 6A in athletics and private schools offering calculus.

*Division 2:

The Division Two competition is for public schools classified as 5A or 4A in athletics and private schools which offer only one year of mathematics beyond both Algebra II with Trigonometry and Geometry

*Division 3:

The Division Three competition is for public schools classified as 3A, 2A, or 1A in athletics and private schools offering no mathematics beyond Algebra II with Trigonometry and Geometry.
